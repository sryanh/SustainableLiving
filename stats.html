Why Go Green?

Global warming has become a heated topic in our society. While scientific evidence continues to be released proving that the earth is heating up many are still skeptical. It is understandable to question whether the earth is really changing or wonder if global warming is just a fear mongering tactic used to distract us from bigger societal issues. However, evidence shows that the earth's temperature is rising at a staggering rate, as well as sea levels and carbon dioxide levels. These changes in the earth's surface effect everything we do.
